# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Rai to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of
background or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behaviour: being respectful, giving and accepting constructive
feedback, and focusing on what's best for the project and community.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal attacks, publishing others' private information, and any conduct that
would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainer at
**abdulrafiu@izusoft.tech**. All complaints will be reviewed and handled fairly and
confidentially.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
